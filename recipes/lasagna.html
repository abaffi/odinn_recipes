Savoury Lasagna

Lasagna is a rich, layered italian dish made with sheets of pasta,savory meat or vegaetable sauce, creamy bechamel and melted cheese, baked until golden and bubbling

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce
  2. Make the cheese mixture
  3. Cook the Lasagna Noodles
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Bake
  6. Serve and enjoy
