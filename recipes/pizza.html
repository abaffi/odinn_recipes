Delicious Pizza

Pizza is a beloved italian dish made of a round,flatbread base topped with savory tomato sauce,melted cheese and assorted toppings baked to perfection

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Bell peppers
  • Olives
  • Mushrooms
  • Oregano
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough
  2. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C)
  3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness
  4. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet
  5. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough
  6. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese over the sauce
  7. Add your favorite toppings such as pepperoni, bell peppers, olives and mushrooms
  8. Sprinkle with oregano and drizzle with olive oil
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving
  11. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
