Delicios Zege

Zege is local Tanzanian dish prefered by street vendos as its easy to make at a low cost

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • onions
  • Eggs
  • cheese
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes and cut them into pieces
  2. Fry the potatoes
  3. when fried add chopped onions
  4. Add eggs and cheese into the fries
  5. Let it cook for 15mnts
  6. Serve and enjoy
